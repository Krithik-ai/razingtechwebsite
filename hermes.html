Have any questions?

What types of chatbots are available?

We offer many different kinds of chatbot services. We offer pre built chatbots for medical and education chatbots. However we also can build your business a custom chatbot that can do things like: book appointments, promote products, and even answer common questions, all with the goal of engaging your customers more.

How long will it take to get my chatbot?

On average it takes around 3-5 business days to analyze your request, create your chatbot, and get your chatbot into your hands. For pre-built chatbots it usually takes less time than a fully custom chatbot.

How can I integrate my chatbot into my business?

We can integrate your chatbot with many apps such as: Instagram, Line, Messenger, Notion, Trello, Webchat, Whatsapp, HTML.

Why should I use a chatbot?

Chatbots are great tools you can use to increase your customer retention, and to attract new customers on your website. There are many benefits to using chatbots, including: Improved Customer Service, Increased Sales Automated Tasks, Reduced Costs, Improved Efficiency
